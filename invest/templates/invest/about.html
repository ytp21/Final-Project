FAQ

How to create a trade record?

Sign in to your account, then select create button on your dashboard and fill up the form. Please use Google Finance to obtain the info required to fill in the Symbol and Market Exchange of a stock.

For example, the symbol and market exchange for Tesla Inc shown in Google Finance are TSLA and NASDAQ respectively. Using these results, input symbol as TSLA and market exchange as NASDAQ in the form to create a trade record for Tesla Inc.

The query on top of the search bar in your Google Finance will result in NASDAQ: TSLA. As a general rule of thumb, the sentence on the left (before the colon) is the market exchange while the sentence on the right (after the colon) is the symbol.

How frequent will the market price and company info be updated?

It will be updated automatically in every 5 minutes
